Kids deserve to live the lives of regular kids having fun and learning new things. This website is meant to help those who would want to explore many programs that the city of chicago has to offer. The violence going on around the city the children need places to keep them preoccupied and this website helps to find those. Not many know of those programs that they may have around their homes. Kids deserve to have the best experiences and meet those who share the same interest. This site is meant to help to serve as a way to branch the kids out.
